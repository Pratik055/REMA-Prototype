 How does the points and rewards system work?
For every OKOlogi product you purchase, you'll earn points that can be accumulated and redeemed for exclusive rewards.
Purchase OKOlogi products to earn points. The points you earn will depend on the product's value and its sustainability level.
Keep track of your points in the "Points Balance" section of your profile.
Browse the "Rewards Catalog" to see available rewards and their point requirements.
Redeem your desired reward by clicking on the "Redeem" button in the catalog.

How to claim your 10% off ØKologi products?
Shop for ØKologi products and add them to your cart.
Proceed to the checkout page.
Apply your 10% discount by clicking on the "Redeem" button.
Complete your purchase and enjoy your savings!
